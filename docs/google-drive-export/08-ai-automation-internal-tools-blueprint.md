# AI Automation & Internal Tools Blueprint

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/15NFzMG9xupwq2k9iLJJ4lb543-GdBoiZse9qXKF7zcg

## Purpose
This document defines the automation systems Pink Halo can build to make the business easier to run.

## Main Automation Goals
Reduce manual work, answer common customer questions, recommend products, recover carts, organize data, and help the owner make better decisions.

## Customer Support AI
The support assistant should answer questions about shipping, returns, sizing, products, discounts, rewards, and order status. It should escalate unusual issues to a human.

## AI Stylist
The AI stylist should help customers choose outfits based on occasion, preferred colors, product category, and available inventory.

## Product Recommendation Engine
The store should recommend matching products, bundles, accessories, and similar items to increase average order value.

## Email Automation
Automated flows should include welcome series, cart recovery, product browse follow-up, purchase confirmation, review request, restock alert, and VIP offer.

## Inventory Alerts
The system should alert when products are low in stock, selling quickly, getting returns, or sitting too long without sales.

## Marketing Automation
Use templates for social captions, product descriptions, email subject lines, product launch posts, and influencer outreach messages.

## Internal Dashboard
The internal dashboard should summarize sales, inventory, product performance, support issues, marketing performance, and tasks.

## Automation Rule
Automation should make the brand faster and more consistent, but it should not make customers feel ignored.
