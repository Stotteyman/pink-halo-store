# Marketing Growth System

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-Z2T5qqGdpVRjew5FNPSueb4oqvfvrsDxO0FIqE8axY

Pink Halo marketing channels include TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest, email, search traffic, creator partnerships, and customer photos.

Core content themes include outfit ideas, new arrivals, packaging videos, customer reviews, style tips, behind the scenes content, limited drops, and seasonal looks.

The launch plan should include brand introduction content, product teasers, collection previews, outfit ideas, daily product highlights, email list growth, creator outreach, and small ad tests.

Pinterest should be treated like a search engine for outfit ideas, seasonal fashion, pink outfit inspiration, dresses, lounge outfits, and gift guides.

Every campaign should track clicks, conversions, orders, revenue, customer feedback, and content performance using real analytics data.
