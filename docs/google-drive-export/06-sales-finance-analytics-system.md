# Sales, Finance & Analytics System

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/13DuY34wTw95gzMD12mmBSiqjDwrAgGOgDAyv3sEw7rw

## Purpose
This document defines how Pink Halo should track money, sales performance, profit, and growth.

## Core Metrics
Revenue, profit, gross margin, average order value, conversion rate, traffic, email subscribers, returning customer rate, refund rate, ad spend, cost per purchase, and best-selling products.

## Simple Profit Formula
Selling price minus product cost minus shipping cost minus packaging cost minus payment fees equals estimated profit.

## Dashboard Sections
Daily sales, weekly sales, monthly sales, best sellers, slow sellers, traffic sources, cart activity, email growth, returns, and customer reviews.

## Budget Categories
Products, samples, packaging, website tools, product photos, ads, creator outreach, shipping supplies, software, and emergency reserve.

## Weekly Review
Check revenue, profit, product performance, marketing results, and customer feedback.

## Decision Rule
Spend more money only on products, channels, or systems that show proof of customer demand.

## Accuracy Rule
All numbers must come from the database and connected analytics systems. No manual/fake production dashboard values.
