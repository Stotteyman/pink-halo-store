# Pink Halo Brand Bible & Identity System

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1RY1GZvA1e1fAbQzP9Je5M-T5T-y_OQ2AyFDrfECJJpk

Pink Halo is a women’s clothing and lifestyle boutique for PinkHalo.co. The brand direction is feminine, dreamy, elegant, warm, and boutique-focused.

Primary colors: pink, blush, champagne gold, pearl white, cream, and deep accent tones.

Official brand phrase: **Wear Your Halo.**

The official logo uses pink script lettering, a glowing halo, and sparkle details. This logo should guide website design, packaging, ads, email templates, social media, and product photography.

## Brand Direction

- Feminine
- Dreamy
- Soft luxury
- Romantic
- Confident
- Elegant
- Warm
- Boutique-focused

## Visual System

- Pink script logo
- Glowing halo
- Sparkle details
- Blush and champagne-gold accents
- Soft, rounded layouts
- Product photography that feels clean and polished
