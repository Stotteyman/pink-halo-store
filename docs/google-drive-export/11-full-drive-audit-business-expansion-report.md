## Audit Status
The Pink Halo Drive is organized as a business operating system instead of a loose storage folder. The numbered structure supports brand, products, website, operations, marketing, finance, customer experience, automation, legal, assets, roadmap, and archive management.

## Folder Accuracy Notes
Each numbered folder owns a distinct business function. Active docs should live in the right folder. Old or duplicate drafts should go to Archive.

## Business Expansion Recommendation
Pink Halo should be developed in phases.

### Phase 1: Foundation
Finalize brand bible, product categories, first collection, website requirements, supplier list, and launch checklist.

### Phase 2: Store Launch
Launch with a focused catalog, clear homepage, simple checkout, customer support policies, email signup, and social media content.

### Phase 3: Growth
Add product bundles, reviews, email flows, Pinterest SEO, creator codes, affiliate tracking, and analytics dashboards.

### Phase 4: Differentiation
Add AI stylist, interactive 3D shopping experience, loyalty program, VIP membership, and limited drops.

### Phase 5: Scale
Add subscription box, mobile app, private-label products, customer community, influencer program, and hiring SOPs.
