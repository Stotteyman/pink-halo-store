# Manufacturer, Fulfillment & Supply Chain Operating System

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1FldTJeMP67kAyul7ZSGSoH5MmJeeok78m5Tw5p-3_k0

## Purpose
Pink Halo should track products from idea to delivery using accurate database records.

## Manufacturer Selection Process
Research, sample order, quality inspection, pricing review, shipping review, communication review, approval, and production.

## Manufacturer Scorecard
Quality, consistency, MOQ, lead time, communication, return handling, packaging quality, shipping speed, and reliability.

## Order Lifecycle
Design approved, manufacturer assigned, purchase order created, production begins, quality control, shipment created, tracking number received, customer notified, delivered, review requested.

## Database Tables
Manufacturers, products, SKUs, purchase orders, customer orders, shipments, tracking numbers, returns, refunds, inventory, warehouses, and suppliers.

## Tracking Rules

- Every shipment must be linked to an order.
- Every order must be linked to a customer.
- Every SKU must be linked to inventory.
- Every inventory movement must be recorded.
- Nothing should rely on manual spreadsheets.

## Admin Dashboard
Orders, revenue, inventory, returns, open shipments, delayed shipments, refunds, popular products, and manufacturer performance.

## Analytics
Gross revenue, net revenue, units sold, inventory levels, average delivery time, return rate, customer satisfaction, and profit margins.

## Data Rule
All numbers displayed must come from database records and live order data, not estimates.
