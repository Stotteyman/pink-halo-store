# 90-Day Launch Roadmap for PinkHalo.co

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/17OouOwjuSGDpneISJL1H0jD-XiuD3JdLhNBWu7_OYNQ

## Purpose
This roadmap gives Pink Halo a clear 90-day path from organized idea to launch-ready online clothing store.

## Main Goal
By the end of 90 days, PinkHalo.co should have a clear brand, first product collection, working website plan, launch content system, customer policies, supplier plan, and operating system.

## Days 1-15: Brand and Product Foundation
Finalize brand bible, confirm product categories, define first collection, choose target customer profiles, write product naming rules, and collect supplier options.

## Days 16-30: Website and Store Setup
Build the core website structure: homepage, shop, product pages, cart, checkout, about, contact, FAQ, shipping, account, and size guide. Make sure the site is mobile-first.

## Days 31-45: Content and Marketing System
Build a 30-day content calendar for TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest, email, and launch countdown posts. Prepare product photo standards and short-form video ideas.

## Days 46-60: Operations and Customer Experience
Prepare order handling, packaging, support scripts, loyalty program, and customer review flow. The store should feel organized before sales begin.

## Days 61-75: Soft Launch
Test the website, checkout, product pages, email signup, discount codes, mobile speed, customer support flow, and first marketing posts. Start collecting early feedback.

## Days 76-90: Public Launch
Launch the store publicly. Push content daily, email the list, post product videos, test small ads, track sales, and review what products and content perform best.

## Success Metrics
First sales, email subscribers, website traffic, conversion rate, average order value, customer feedback, social engagement, and repeat visits.

## Next Phase After 90 Days
Add loyalty rewards, influencer codes, product bundles, AI stylist, 3D shopping experience, subscription box planning, and private-label product development.
