# Pink Halo Social Media Advertising Plan

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1oZReVIsz8hbrKt-8_iJSNa5AsJfAx8UUiYp-sNFvJ-0

This file mirrors the Google Drive social media advertising plan into GitHub.

## Purpose
Plan paid and organic promotion for Pink Halo across social platforms.

## Key Channels

- TikTok
- Instagram
- Pinterest
- Email
- Creator partnerships

## Advertising Direction
Pink Halo ads should focus on feminine fashion, outfit confidence, pretty product visuals, collection drops, bundles, and social proof.

## Tracking Rule
Ad reporting should connect to real analytics, real campaign data, and database-backed order attribution.
