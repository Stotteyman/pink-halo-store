# Customer Experience, Support & Loyalty System

Source Google Drive Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1R-1BcjuH7ReaRNZxHwjWmJjLciLohFZpLEpl_fPNSSg

## Purpose
This document defines how Pink Halo should treat customers before, during, and after purchase.

## Customer Experience Goal
Every customer should feel helped, respected, and excited to receive their order.

## Support Tone
Warm, clear, polite, helpful, and brand-aligned. Messages should be simple and direct while still feeling personal.

## Common Support Topics
Order status, shipping time, tracking, returns, exchanges, sizing, product questions, discounts, payments, and restock requests.

## Support Reply Formula

1. Acknowledge the customer.
2. Explain the answer clearly.
3. Give the next step.
4. End with a warm closing.

## Loyalty Program
Pink Halo Rewards should give points for purchases, reviews, referrals, birthdays, joining the email list, sharing photos, and repeat orders.

## Reward Ideas
Discounts, free shipping, early access, VIP drops, birthday reward, and special bundle offers.

## Customer Retention
Customers should be encouraged to join the email list, follow social media, create an account, save wishlist items, and return for seasonal drops.

## Experience Standard
Customers should never feel ignored. Quick, kind, and clear communication is part of the brand.
