This file mirrors the Google Drive operations manual for GitHub availability.

## Purpose
This manual explains how Pink Halo should run day to day.

## Daily Checklist
Check new orders, review customer messages, inspect inventory alerts, update tracking numbers, review social media comments, and confirm supplier issues.

## Core Workflows

- Order processing
- Package preparation
- Customer support replies
- Product issue review
- Inventory review
- Supplier follow-up
- Weekly reporting
- Product launch workflow
- Content posting workflow

## Quality Control
Before fulfillment, check that the correct product, color, size, quantity, and packaging are prepared.

## Customer Issue Log
Repeated issues should be logged. If the same issue appears more than twice, create a better FAQ answer, product note, support script, or website improvement.

## Weekly Operations Meeting Agenda
Review orders, customer questions, inventory, supplier concerns, website problems, marketing tasks, and next week priorities.

## Hiring Readiness
Every task that may later be handed to a helper should have a short SOP. The goal is to make Pink Halo easy to operate even when work is delegated.
