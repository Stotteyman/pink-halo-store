# Pink Halo Product Pipeline

## Purpose
This document defines how Pink Halo turns ideas into products that can be sold, manufactured, tracked, shipped, and analyzed.

## Pipeline

```text
Idea
→ Design
→ Mockup
→ Approval
→ Manufacturer Selection
→ Sample
→ Quality Control
→ Inventory Plan
→ Product Listing
→ Marketing Assets
→ Launch
→ Orders
→ Shipping
→ Analytics
→ Restock, Improve, or Retire
```

## Product Creation Requirements

Every product should have:

- Product name
- Collection
- Category
- SKU
- Variant options
- Design or mockup link
- Manufacturer or supplier
- Cost
- Selling price
- Margin estimate
- Inventory plan
- Product images
- Product description
- Launch date
- Status

## Product Statuses

```text

```

## Decision Gates

### Design Gate
Does this match Pink Halo’s brand?

### Margin Gate
Can this make enough profit after product cost, shipping, packaging, and fees?

### Manufacturer Gate
Can the manufacturer deliver consistent quality?

### Listing Gate
Does the product have strong photos, copy, size info, and shipping info?

### Launch Gate
Is there enough content ready to promote the product?

## Analytics After Launch

Track:

- Views
- Add-to-cart rate
- Sales
- Conversion rate
- Return rate
- Reviews
- Profit by SKU
- Customer feedback
- Content performance

## Restock Rule

Restock products that prove demand, fit the brand, have healthy margins, and receive good feedback.
